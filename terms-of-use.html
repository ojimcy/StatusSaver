Section 06

Notification Access (Android)

The deleted message recovery feature, available exclusively on Android, requires the Notification Listener Service permission. This section explains how this permission is used.

What This Permission Does

When enabled, the App receives a copy of notifications posted to your device's notification shade. This allows the App to capture the text content of incoming WhatsApp messages at the moment they appear as notifications, before they can be deleted by the sender.

What Data Is Captured

  • The sender's name or contact identifier as displayed in the notification.
  • The text content of the message as it appears in the notification.
  • The timestamp of when the notification was received.

What Data Is NOT Captured

  • Media attachments (images, videos, voice notes, documents) are not captured through notifications.
  • Notifications from apps other than WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business are ignored and discarded.
  • End-to-end encrypted message content beyond what is displayed in notifications is not accessed.

How Data Is Handled

  • All captured notification data is stored locally on your device only.
  • No captured data is transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party.
  • You may delete captured messages at any time from within the App.
  • Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.
Your Control

Notification access is entirely optional. The App's other features (status saving, favorites) function independently without this permission. You may revoke notification access at any time through your device's system settings.
